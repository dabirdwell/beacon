Beacon

One-button live streaming for communities and civic groups.

What it does

Beacon lets anyone go live from their phone with a single tap. No accounts, no app stores, no configuration for the person streaming. One technical person sets it up on a server; everyone else just opens a link and taps GO LIVE.

Who it's for

Neighborhood watch groups. Civic organizations. Community monitors. Anyone who needs a fast, reliable way to share what's happening — on infrastructure they control.

How it works

  1. Set up Beacon on any server with Docker (bash setup.sh)
  2. Open the streamer URL on your phone
  3. Tap GO LIVE
  4. Share the viewer link — anyone can watch in their browser

Self-hosted. Open source. Free.

Beacon runs on your server. Your stream goes through your infrastructure. No third-party dependencies, no platform risk, no data collection. MIT licensed.
